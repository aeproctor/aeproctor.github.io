Membership

Why Join?

The best way to learn more about Marshfield's history and support our organization is to join! When you join the Marshfield Historical Society, you are supporting all our endeavors. This includes the restoration and upkeep of both the Marcia Thomas House and the Winslow Schoolhouse. Your membership fee also helps us to purchase more artifacts for our museum. All members also qualify for discounted entry prices to our Pro-Am Lectures.

Join today and help us preserve the history of our beloved town!
